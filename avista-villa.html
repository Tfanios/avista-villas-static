The house

A house built
for light.

Set into the hillside above Vourvourou, Avista Villa opens completely to the view. Living spaces flow without walls to the edge of the pool, where the water meets the horizon — and five bedrooms, including a step-free ground-floor suite, sit in the quiet of the pines.

5 bedrooms
Each en-suite, including a step-free ground-floor suite.
Sleeps 10
Comfortable for a family or group of ten.
Infinity edge pool
A heated, horizon-edge pool the length of the terrace.
360° sea views
The Aegean in frame from nearly every room.
Walls of glass
Floor-to-ceiling glass; inside and terrace become one.
Chef-ready kitchen
A full kitchen and a long table for slow dinners.
Air-conditioned
Blackout and climate control throughout.
Fast Wi-Fi
Reliable connection across the house.
