The house

A house built for light, and for long days by the water.

Set into the hillside above Vourvourou, Avista Villa opens completely to the view. Living spaces flow without walls from the kitchen to the terrace to the edge of the pool, where the water meets the horizon. Five bedrooms, each with its own character; a ground-floor suite designed for easy, step-free access; and the quiet of the pines all around.
