The Collection

Two distinct houses, one shoreline. Avista is a private retreat for those who want the whole place to themselves.

Set among Aleppo pines on the Sithonia peninsula, the villas look out over the sheltered waters of Vourvourou and the scattered islets of Diaporos. Each is offered exclusively — your family, your guests, and no one else.
